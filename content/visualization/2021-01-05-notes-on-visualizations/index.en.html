Overplotting may distort scatter plots

Scatter plots may have problems with overplotting (Tidyverse 2021) when one point is plotted on top of another. Overplotting distort the information shown, accordingly.

Approaches by GGPLOT to mitigate overplotting include using

  • geom_smooth(),
  • geom_quantile(),
  • geom_density_2d(),
  • geom_boxplot(),
  • geom_count(),
  • geom_hex(), or
  • geom_density2d().

References

Tidyverse. 2021. “Ggplot2: Points.” https://ggplot2.tidyverse.org/reference/geom_point.html.

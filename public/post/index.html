The Binomial Theorem

The binomial theorem states for real or complex $a, b,$ and non-negative $n$, \begin{equation} (a+b)^n = \sum^n_{k=0} {n \choose k} a^{n-k}b^k \end{equation} where ${n \choose k} = \frac {n!}{k!(n-k)!}$ is a binomial coefficient.

Last updated on Dec 24, 2020

The question here is, how to add an entirely new page section Z to your Hugo-Academic website. This is to say that you want to add the following components to your site:

  • A new directory to hold a set of pages on topic Z
  • A menu reference that links to your list of pages on topic Z
  • A panel section on your home page specific to your posts on topic Z
  • A index file inside of your new directory of pages on topic Z
